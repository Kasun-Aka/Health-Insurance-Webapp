Terms and Conditions for TrueLife Insurance Policy

Minimum Commitment & Cancellation

  • A minimum commitment period of **3 months** applies to all policies.
  • Cancellation before the 3-month period incurs a penalty equal to one month's premium.
  • After 3 months, cancellation requires a 30-day notice period.

Premium & Payments

  • The monthly premium rate is fixed for the duration specified in the policy details.
  • Late payment (over 7 days) may result in temporary suspension of benefits.

Claim Limits & Exclusions

  • All claims are subject to verification and policy limits specified in the full contract.
  • Pre-existing conditions not disclosed during sign-up are generally **excluded** from coverage.
